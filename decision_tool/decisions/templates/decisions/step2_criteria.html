Why set a realistic range? Without a range, the system stretches the gap between your candidates to fill the full scale. If two candidates have 4 and 5 years experience, that 1-year gap becomes the entire difference between 0% and 100%. Setting Min = 0, Max = 10 means 4 years = 40% and 5 years = 50% — a fair, proportionate gap. Leave blank to use the default (auto-scaled from candidates).
